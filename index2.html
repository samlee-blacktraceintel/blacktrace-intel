About Us

BlackTrace Intel is a veteran-owned cyber intelligence company operating at the intersection of national security and offensive cybersecurity. Founded by a U.S. Marine Corps veteran and seasoned penetration tester, BlackTrace exists to provide elite-level reconnaissance, reverse engineering, and threat detection capabilities for mission-critical operations.

With deep roots in red team operations, vulnerability research, and embedded systems exploitation, we approach each engagement with tactical precision, discretion, and a clear objective: to outmaneuver cyber adversaries before they strike.

Services

  • Cyber Threat Intelligence: Actionable threat insights tailored to your operational risk profile.
  • Red Team Operations: Simulated adversarial attacks to assess and improve cyber resilience.
  • Exploit Development: Custom exploit design and deployment against zero-day and known vulnerabilities.
  • Advanced Reconnaissance: Covert cyber and signal data collection for mission planning and attribution.
  • Reverse Engineering: Firmware and malware dissection to expose vulnerabilities and tampering.
  • Penetration Testing: Targeted assessments of networks, applications, and embedded systems.

Case Studies

  • Operation Phantom Trace: Identified GPS firmware vulnerabilities used in spoofing campaigns.
  • Red Skies Assessment: Reduced lateral movement exposure for critical infrastructure by 60%.
  • SIGINT Exploit Chain: Developed radio protocol exploit chains in joint R&D with federal partners.
